Desk Height : -- to --

Keyboard and mouse should placed in line with the elbows. Thus, the upper body is not permanently stressed on one side, which prevents cramps and disharmonies in the shoulder, back and neck musculature. Use hand supports! These prevent bending of the hands and gentle joints, tendons and nerves from the hand to the elbow.

Monitor Height : --

The monitor should be placed below the horizontal visual axis and should be, at least, one arms length away. If the screen is too close or at the wrong height, the body is often tense. This creates tension in the shoulder and neck area.
